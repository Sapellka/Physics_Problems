# Task 10 – Kinematics

## Step-by-Step Solution

Trajectory:

$$
\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1
$$

Helical motion due to $z=bt$.

Velocity:

$$
\vec{v} = (-a\omega \sin\omega t, b\omega \cos\omega t, b)
$$

Speed:

$$
|\vec{v}| = \sqrt{a^2 \omega^2 \sin^2 + b^2 \omega^2 \cos^2 + b^2}
$$

## Final Result

Trajectory is a helix.

## Interpretation

Combination of circular and linear motion.
