# Task 05 – Relative Velocity

## Problem Statement

Boat crossing a river with current.

## Step-by-Step Solution

Let angle $\theta$ west of north.

Condition: horizontal velocity cancels river:

$$
5 \sin\theta = 2
$$

$$
\sin\theta = \frac{2}{5}
$$

$$
\theta \approx 23.6^\circ
$$

Vertical velocity:

$$
v_y = 5 \cos\theta \approx 4.58
$$

Time:

$$
t = \frac{200}{4.58} \approx 43.7 \ \text{s}
$$

## Final Result

- Angle: $23.6^\circ$ west of north  
- Time: $\approx 43.7$ s  

## Interpretation

Boat must aim upstream to cancel drift.
