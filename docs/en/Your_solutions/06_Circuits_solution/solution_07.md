# Task 07 – Capacitors in Parallel

## Problem Statement

Two capacitors,

$$
C_1 = 4\,\mu\text{F}
$$

and

$$
C_2 = 6\,\mu\text{F}
$$

are connected in parallel to a

$$
10 \text{ V}
$$

battery.

Determine:

- the total charge stored,
- the total energy stored.

## Theory

For capacitors in parallel,

$$
C_{\text{eq}} = C_1 + C_2
$$

The total charge is

$$
Q = C_{\text{eq}} V
$$

The total stored energy is

$$
W = \frac{1}{2} C_{\text{eq}} V^2
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

### 1. Equivalent capacitance

$$
C_{\text{eq}} = 4\,\mu\text{F} + 6\,\mu\text{F} = 10\,\mu\text{F}
$$

$$
C_{\text{eq}} = 10 \times 10^{-6} \text{ F}
$$

### 2. Total charge

$$
Q = C_{\text{eq}} V = (10 \times 10^{-6})(10)
$$

$$
Q = 100 \times 10^{-6} \text{ C} = 100\,\mu\text{C}
$$

### 3. Total energy

$$
W = \frac{1}{2} C_{\text{eq}} V^2
$$

$$
W = \frac{1}{2}(10 \times 10^{-6})(10^2)
$$

$$
W = 5.0 \times 10^{-4} \text{ J}
$$

## Final Result

The total charge stored is

$$
Q = 100\,\mu\text{C}
$$

The total energy stored is

$$
W = 5.0 \times 10^{-4} \text{ J}
$$
